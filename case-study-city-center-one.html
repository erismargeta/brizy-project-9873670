Case Study: CITY CENTER ONE

We are excited to announce that we have just finished a remarkably successful influencer marketing campaign with the biggest shopping mall in Croatia - City Center One

Take a deep dive into our process.

Table of contents:

  • Part 1 - INTO ACTION!!!
  • Hypefy x CCO
  • Part 2 - AI Magic
  • Part 3 - Diamond Grinding 
  • Part 4 - final work and great results

Part 1 - INTO ACTION!!!


First - we needed to define clear goals with our client. Our campaigns can be focused on three things:

  • reach
  • brand recognition
  • conversion (sales)

In each of the categories, we excel at providing unmatched media value to our clients. What's media value you ask? 

Simply put, it's how many views per dollar (or conversions per dollar) you get - compared to traditional marketing channels.>

Influencer marketing campaign goal - reach (maximize views and engagement)

Right after the first meeting with City Center marketing team we started brainstorming and came up with some brilliant ideas.

Theme of the campaign, Spring/summer collection 2022., made it quite easy to create real and organic Hype among influencers who were all extremely excited about working with Hypefy & CCO.  


Hypefy & CCO 

City Center One came in Croatia in 2006. with their first shopping mall City Center West. Today there are three malls, two in Zagreb and one in Split.

After twelve years of running a successful business, we came to refresh the already high-quality influencer marketing they had. By merging their long-standing relationships with a few of big influencer names and a battalion of some fresh new influencers that were the choice of our AI algorithm, we made a hyped-up set of competing influencers to start the campaign.

The Great Comparison 🔥⚔️

It was actually a competition. By the wish of our client - we had two competing campaigns running simultaneously. It was now or never - CCO vs. HYPEFY

CCO in the left corner with their in-house influencers and already proven process

vs.

HYPEFY in the right corner with our AI algorithm and the power of hype on our side

We needed to win this. Luckily, we have magic on our side. 😎


Part 2 - AI Magic

We identified 109 influencers from our database which would match the City Center One budget and market parameters. Top 19 of them were considered and 11 engaged.

On this campaign our goal was to spread the message to the highest possible number of unique followers for the least amount of money. 

We put all the parameters into our AI platform> and, just like that, we identified 109 influencers from our database which would match the City Center budget and market parameters. 

We fine-tuned the campaign parameters to reach as many diverse groups as possible. Here, we focused on finding the best micro and nano influencers who INSPIRE others with their style.

Few big influencers vs. a lot of smaller ones

Micro and nano influencers, on average have a higher engagement rate with their audience. Most of the time, their audience are the people who are intimately engaged and interested in their favorite influencers' niche taste and lifestyle - in this case, we targeted clothing, fashion and lifestyle influencers with a unique flare.

Part 3 - Diamond Grinding 

Micro and nano influencers, on average have a higher engagement rate with their audience. Most of the time, their audience are the people who are intimately engaged and interested in their favorite influencers' niche taste and lifestyle - in this case, we targeted clothing, fashion and lifestyle influencers with a unique flare.

As we wanted the best out of all the great matches, we considered top 19 of them and made a deal with 11.

Influencers we chose have one thing in common - audience that is interested in their specific style and creative outfits - and want to know where they buy clothes. (influencers often get messages about buying recommendations)

That was the perfect choice and we had not one, but eleven girls we wanted to work with, and they also wanted to work with us.💜 Our algorithm helped us by optimizing various campaign aspects, so that we knew exactly how much money we will pay each influencer.

All proposals were sent automatically through our platform, and the campaign started right after all influencers accepted it.

At this phase - our platform automatically does many things:

  • analysis - once again, the chosen profiles are inputted into our platform, and this time they go through a thorough analysis. Fake followers, fake likes, inflated engagement and all the other red flags are immediately recognised here.
  • budgeting - which influencer is worth exactly how much money, based on media value and depending on the analysis of their social media profiles.
  • content approval - all of influencer's content is uploaded to our platform and is either approved or rejected by us or the client
  • campaign post scheduling - out of tens of influencers weekly, depending on each one's performance every day, we get suggestions which ones need to publish their posts the next day, and they get notified through our platform exactly when to post their content
  • active monitoring - all of influencers' content is monitored during each campaign to avoid any red flag behaviour such as posting fake giveaways, paid advertising, fake likes and engagement, etc...
  • data collection - all influencers upload their private instagram dashboard data (not accessible through API's) to our platform, where it is then parsed, checked for anomalies and stored for later processing
  • reporting - at the end of each campaign, all gathered data is processed and a detailed report is generated. The report contains very detailed information about each influencer's performance as well as data on different campaign parts. For example, a campaign can include testing of some new product in different demographic groups - this in turn produces extremely valuable data as it is first-hand, real-world feedback on product's performance in that target demographic.  If multiple campaigns are run at the same time, our report includes a direct comparison which highlights differences and big winners for a step-up next month (if a monthly recurring plan is chosen with Hypefy - check our pricing for enterprise solutions-> https://www.hypefy.ai/pricing>)

Final work and great results

The entire process went very fluently. We gave some ideas to influencers, what can they film, how can reel look like etc but we let them be their selves because we believe they are doing fantastic job and make the best content on their authentic way. It took only ten days from the signing of the agreement to the publication of the first content on Instagram. Girls were doing an excellent job and put all their content on platform which we sent directly to CCO, not a single content required changes. For an initial investment, CCO received 220% more euros in media value.
