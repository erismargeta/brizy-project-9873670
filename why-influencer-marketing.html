Why influencer marketing?

Influencer marketing uses the reach, authenticity and personality of individuals who have built up their own following in a specific niche with a particular target audience.

Why not paid ads or different channels?


Ad targeting can help you target a lot of things about your audience, but it doesn't give you the flexibility to select the audience niche.

Influencers already have their established followers and a great connection with them.

When influencers ask their followers to check some service or product, they are more likely to react and believe in the same.

Return on investment (ROI)

Difficulties

Finding and contacting influencers, content creating and scheduling for the maximum effect can be very tricky. Other difficulties are brand recognition, quality control and real time report which can rarely be on time. And last but not least, paying every influencer individually can take a lot of time and effort.
